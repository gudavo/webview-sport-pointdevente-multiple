How to play ?

How to place a bet?

In France, betting odds are expressed as decimals, not fractions. Converting from fractional to decimal odds is easy. Just add “1” to the fraction.
Here are a few sample conversions:

Comparison table between fractional and decimal odds
Fractional odds Decimal odds
1/5 1,2
1/2 1,5
1/1 2
2/1 3

“1N2” Bet

If you correctly predict the outcome, you win. The amount you win is equal to the amount of your bet multiplied by the odds of the prediction.

Select your match and your prediction:

  • 1First team wins
  • NDraw between the 2 teams
  • 2Second team wins

Example

England - Argentina

2023 RWC

N°34 1/N/2 I Valid until 09/09 8:55 PM

+150 bets
  • 1 1,35
  • N 20,00
  • 2 3,00

If you bet €10 on England winning, you’d win €13.50 (€10 x 1,35)

Click on “+150 bets” to see a wider range of options with new bets on the same match.

“1N2 Handicap” Bet

In this type of bet, which is based on the score at the end of regulation time, a handicap is assigned to one of the two teams, giving the other team a several-point lead over the one with the handicap.

Example

England - Argentina

2023 RWC

1/N/2 Handicap [0:7]

  • 1 1,80
  • N 20
  • 2 1,80

How to read this bet:

  • 1 England wins by at least 8 points (i.e. strictly more than 7 points)
  • N England wins by exactly 7 points
  • 2 Argentina wins, or draw, or England wins by 6 points or less (i.e. strictly less than 7 points)

How to validate your bets?

Validate your bets at a point of sale up to 5 minutes before the match

  1. Place your bet, and top on the button to access the cart.

  2. Enter your chips and tap on “Valider” to convert your selection into a QR code.

  3. Go to a Parions Sport point of sale. Open your app and access the “Mes QR codes” section in your cart. Tap on the “QR code” button under your bet.

  4. Show the QR code of your bet to the salesperson.

  5. You’ll given a bet receipt, which you’ll need to show to collect your winnings.

Collect your winnings in cash for amounts up to €300 at any FDJ point of sale and via bak transfer for sums exceeding this amount.
